Who We Are

Esprit International School is an accredited Montessori and authorized
IB World School. We were founded with a dedication to providing
children a world-class education of the mind and heart. Our goal is to
nurture self-confidence, encourage personal creativity, and support the
entrepreneurial spirit in the growing child. Students at Esprit proceed
through an exciting and challenging course of studies at a highly
individualized pace. Esprit is a school with a rigorous, yet innovative,
academic program. We offer a supportive academic atmosphere, yet set
a high level of expectation for the quality of thought, work, and mastery
of content and skills within each program.
